Sending a transfer


Simply visit any BB branch with a valid identity card (passport/driver's license/voter's ID). Fill out an Xpress transfer form and hand it to the customer service representative. You will be provided with a receipt and secure code as proof of transfer

Receiving a transfer


Visit the Specified BB branch with your valid identity card(passport/driver's license/voter's ID) and the secure code received from the sender. Fill out the Xpress receiver form and hand it to the customer service representative to receive the funds.
